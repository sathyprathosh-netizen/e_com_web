Lehenga Style Guide

Which lehenga is right for your occasion?

👑 Bridal Lehenga
Heavy silk or velvet with zardozi, kundan and zari embroidery. Choose deep reds, maroons, and golds for that royal look.
🎉 Festive Lehenga
Georgette or net with mirror work, gota patti or printed designs. Perfect for Navratri, Diwali, and family celebrations.
🎆 Party Lehenga
Sequined net or embellished fabrics in pastels or bold colours. Perfect for sangeet nights, cocktail parties and receptions.
